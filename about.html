The Collection

Restored to drive.
Not to store.

KV Classic Rentals is a curated collection of hand-restored classics — available to rent for weddings, special occasions, milestone drives, and anything else that deserves an extraordinary car.

Every car in the fleet comes out of KV Classics, a restoration workshop built on a straightforward belief: these vehicles were made to be driven, and they deserve to look exactly as good as the day they were built.

The collection rotates as new restorations are completed. Each car is here because it earned its place — chosen for its character, its condition, and the feeling it creates. Right now there are two cars available, with a third arriving shortly.
